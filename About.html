About Us

You might be unsure of which charities you’d like to support, but you’re passionate about the urgent issues of our time.On our platform, you can donate to a non-profit with confidence. Our robust due diligence process covers legal, compliance, financial and impact checks for each donation made to any non-profit, including in-person visits to beneficiaries and actual feedback reports.

  3. Convenience: Contribute monthly with ease
  4. Credible: Donate with confidence to the verified partner NGOs
  5. Scale: Make a big impact with your small possible amounts.
